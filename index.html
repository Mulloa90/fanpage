Biography

Matthew Dean Burdette (born September 17, 1992), known professionally as Blxst (pronounced "blast"),

is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer from Los Angeles.

His record label Evgle partnered with Red Bull Records for the release of his debut EP No Love Lost on September 4, 2020,

spawning the hit single "Chosen" featuring Tyga and Ty Dolla Sign. He released his debut album, Before You Go on April 22, 2022.
